Who We Are

Challenge Law Firm is established in 2011 with an aim to serve the clients in an organized manner to achieve the best outcome for every client. The Partners of Challenge Law Firm got together after getting exposure in various fields of law i.e. Antitrust, Aviation, Litigation, Tax, Arbitration, Corporate, IP laws, Labor, Immigration, Drafting Contracts and various other legal fields During the past few years Challenge Law Firm has succeeded to be one of the top ten leading law firms in Egypt due to loyalty and quality of work delivered.
